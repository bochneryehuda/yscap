Two ways to move

Fix & Flip vs Bridge

Both are short-term and asset-based. The difference is whether you’re renovating to resell, or acquiring to stabilize.

Renovate to resell

Fix & Flip

Short-term rehab capital. Up to 90% of the purchase price and 100% of the renovation budget, released in draws as work is verified — up to 95% LTC, up to 75% of after-repair value (ARV), up to 92.5% loan-to-cost. 1 pt origination.

  • Use it when
  • You’re buying a value-add property and funding the rehab
  • The exit is a resale at the after-repair value
  • You want the renovation financed and drawn as work completes

Acquire & stabilize

Bridge

Fast, asset-based financing to acquire or stabilize now and refinance or sell later. Limited docs — compete with cash.

  • Use it when
  • You need to close quickly and can’t wait on full-doc financing
  • The property needs to be stabilized before a longer-term loan
  • Your exit is a refinance or a sale down the road

The mechanics

How draws work

The renovation is financed and released in stages — not handed over up front. You draw against completed, verified work.

A dedicated draw coordinator manages the schedule, so capital moves as fast as the work does.

  • Fund the purchaseClose on the acquisition with up to 90% of the purchase price financed.
  • Complete a stage of workRun the renovation on your schedule — 100% of the budget is available across draws.
  • Request a drawSubmit for the completed work; your dedicated draw coordinator manages the request.
  • Verify and releaseOnce the work is verified, the draw is released — then repeat for the next stage.

FAQ

Questions we hear most

How is the renovation funded?+
The renovation is financed and released in draws as work is verified — up to 100% of the budget. A dedicated draw coordinator manages the draw schedule so capital moves as the work completes.
What’s the difference between Fix & Flip and Bridge?+
Fix & Flip is short-term rehab capital for buying and renovating to resell, with the renovation funded in draws. Bridge is fast, asset-based financing to acquire or stabilize now and refinance or sell later, with limited docs so you can compete with cash.
