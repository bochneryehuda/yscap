The mechanics

How DSCR works

DSCR = Property rent ÷ PITIA

Debt-Service Coverage Ratio measures the property’s rent against its full housing payment — principal, interest, taxes, insurance and any association dues.

A DSCR of 1.0 or above typically qualifies, because the rent is expected to cover the payment. Your personal income never enters the calculation.

  • Start with the rentWe use the property’s market or in-place rent as the income line — not your paystub.
  • Divide by the paymentRent is measured against PITIA to produce the coverage ratio.
  • Clear the thresholdA ratio of 1.0 or above generally qualifies the loan on the asset’s own cash flow.
  • Skip the paperworkNo tax returns, no W-2s, no personal income documentation to chase.

FAQ

Questions we hear most

Do you check my income?+
No — the loan is qualified on the property, not on you. DSCR financing is business-purpose lending underwritten to the asset’s cash flow, so there are no tax returns or W-2s to provide.
What’s the minimum FICO?+
Representative FICO from 600, and first-time investors are welcome. Your score is one input; the property’s coverage does the heavy lifting.
Is this a consumer or business loan?+
All YS Capital lending is business/investment-purpose on non-owner-occupied property. A personal guaranty and first-lien position are standard.
