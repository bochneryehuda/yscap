Structure

Your rehab budget is financed 100% and held back in draws — separate from any interest reserve. Leave the reserve at 0 to keep the loan to acquisition + rehab; enter months to finance a reserve (it tracks your real rate and must fit under the leverage caps).

A bridge loan is sized against the as-is value only — there's no rehab holdback, after-repair value or interest reserve. Just set your requested term below.

    How this loan amount is built

    Your maximum is the lesser of four limits — the tightest one wins:

    1. Initial advance — up to your max as-is % of the lower of purchase price or as-is value.
    2. Rehab — 100% financed, released in draws as work is verified and reimbursed after completion.
    3. Loan-to-cost — total can't exceed your max LTC of purchase + rehab.
    4. Loan-to-ARV — total can't exceed your max % of the after-repair value.
