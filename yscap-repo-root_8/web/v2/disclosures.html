Business purpose only. All mortgage products and information on this website, and every tool in the Investor Suite, are intended solely for business-purpose and investment (non-owner-occupied) properties. We do not offer or accept applications for consumer-purpose loans (personal, family or household use) through this platform. By using this site you certify that any inquiry is for a commercial or business investment.

Rates, leverage & pricing are indicative. Any rate, leverage (LTV/LTC/ARV), fee, closing-cost, or loan-amount figure shown anywhere on this website or in our tools is illustrative and indicative only — a representative example, not an APR, not a rate lock, and not an offer or commitment to lend. Your actual terms depend on loan type, credit (FICO), leverage, DSCR, property type, occupancy, loan amount, points elected, prepayment structure, experience, state, and full underwriting, and may differ. Leverage shown is a maximum, set by experience and credit and capped by property value/ARV. Funding timelines vary by deal, title and third-party turnaround.

Investor tools & estimates. The calculators and tools in the Investor Suite (deal, flip, equity, refinance, portfolio, rate, qualifier, rehab budget, track record and term-sheet tools) are provided for estimation and education only. Every output is an estimate based on the values you enter and is not a quote, an approval, or a commitment to lend, nor financial, legal, accounting or tax advice. Use is at your own risk; verify all figures with your loan team before relying on them.

Term sheets & proposals. A term sheet or scenario generated here is an illustrative, indicative proposal only and is not a commitment to lend, a pre-approval, or a rate lock. Every program carries a minimum earned-interest provision of three months — an interest floor (not a prepayment penalty): if the loan pays off before three full months of interest have been earned, the remaining balance of that minimum interest is due at payoff. Final terms are set by the executed loan documents after full underwriting.

That portal is operated by Sitewire, not by YS Capital Group, and its own terms and privacy practices govern information you submit there.
